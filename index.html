Welcome to the Kingland!

Yernar Shambayev was born in the former Soviet Union and lives in Kazakhstan — a background that gives him a unique perspective on the cultural and political journey of Stephen King’s work behind the Iron Curtain. He is a writer, literary translator, and English teacher. His translation projects include works by Pulitzer Prize–winning author Donna Tartt. Stephen King Behind the Iron Curtain is his latest nonfiction book, blending literary history, Cold War and contemporary politics, deep knowledge of Russian publishing, and personal passion.
