Stephen King Behind the Iron Curtain

Stephen King has terrified and thrilled readers around the world, but few know what happened when his books crossed the Iron Curtain.

This nonfiction book explores the extraordinary story of Stephen King's books in the Soviet Union and Russia from 1981 to 2025, including censorship, unofficial translations, publishing history, fan culture, and the surprising impact of King's fiction on Soviet and Russian readers.
